# Social Media Painpoint Research

## The Prompt

```
I'm looking for direct quotes and descriptions of painpoints of [ICP]. Focus on social media data sources.
```

## How to Use

Replace `[ICP]` with your ideal customer profile. Be specific about who you're researching.

## ICP Examples

```
<!-- Example 1: Family Caregivers -->
painpoints of family caregivers, the sandwich generation, who are taking care of elderly parents

<!-- Example 2: Solo Founders -->
painpoints of solo founders building their first SaaS product without a technical background

<!-- Example 3: Freelance Designers -->
painpoints of freelance graphic designers trying to find consistent client work

<!-- Example 4: Remote Workers -->
painpoints of remote workers struggling with work-life boundaries and isolation

<!-- Example 5: First-Time Managers -->
painpoints of individual contributors who just got promoted to management roles

<!-- Example 6: Small Business Owners -->
painpoints of small retail business owners competing with Amazon

<!-- Example 7: Content Creators -->
painpoints of YouTube creators trying to monetize with under 10K subscribers

<!-- Example 8: Career Changers -->
painpoints of professionals transitioning from corporate jobs to entrepreneurship

<!-- Example 9: New Parents -->
painpoints of new parents trying to maintain their careers while caring for infants

<!-- Example 10: Indie App Developers -->
painpoints of indie mobile app developers trying to get discovered in app stores
```

## Tips

- Be as specific as possible with your ICP description
- Ask AI to pull from Reddit, Twitter/X, forums, Facebook groups
- Look for direct quotes and emotional language
- Identify patterns across multiple sources

## What You'll Get

- Real customer language (use this in your copy)
- Validation of problem severity
- Insights into what solutions they've already tried
- Understanding of emotional impact
